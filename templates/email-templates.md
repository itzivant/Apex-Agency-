# Email & SMS Templates — Reference for outreach-writer

These are reference patterns. Don't copy verbatim. Use as structural guidance.

## Cold email — "no website" angle
Subject: Quick note about {Business} site
Hey {first name},
I noticed {Business} doesn't have a website yet, but you've got {N} reviews on Google — people clearly love you. Most folks searching "{niche} {city}" today are clicking the first result with a website. You're losing those calls to {known competitor or "the guys with worse reviews"}.
I build sites for local trades. Takes about 2 weeks, ${price}. Any chance for a 10-min call this week?
— Ivan, {phone}

## Cold email — "outdated site" angle  
Subject: {Business}'s website
Hey {first name},
Took a look at {Business}'s site — it works, but it looks like it was built around {year-ish}. New customers checking you out on their phone probably bounce. You're losing leads you're already paying to attract.
Quick rebuild runs ${price}, takes ~2 weeks. 10 minutes on the phone — worth it?
— Ivan, {phone}

## SMS — short
Hey {first name}, Ivan here — local web guy in Madera. Saw {Business} doesn't have a site. Build one in 2 weeks for ${price}. 10-min call this week?

## Voicemail — script
Hey {first name}, Ivan calling — I build websites for plumbers and contractors out here in the Valley. I noticed {Business} doesn't have one yet and figured you might be losing calls to it. Total no-pressure — give me a buzz back at {phone} if you want to chat. Talk soon.

## Follow-up #1 — bump
Hey {first name}, just bumping this in case it got buried. Still happy to chat about that website if you're interested. — Ivan

## Follow-up #2 — different angle (social proof)
Hey {first name} — I just finished a site for {comparable business in nearby city} and they booked {N} jobs in the first month from it. Same setup would work for {Business}. Worth a 10-min call?

## Breakup
Hey {first name}, I'll stop following up — I'm sure you've got a lot going on. If you ever want to revisit, you've got my number. Take care. — Ivan

## Reply to "send me more info"
Hey {first name}, sure thing. Quick rundown:
- {Package name}: {what's included} — ${price} one-time + ${monthly}/mo
- Timeline: ~2 weeks from deposit
- 50% deposit, 50% on launch
- Includes 2 rounds of revisions per page
Want to jump on a quick call so I can ask a few questions about what you actually need? — Ivan

## Reply to "what's the price?"
{Package} runs ${price}. Includes {key features}. Most clients are launched in 2 weeks. Want to hop on a quick call to make sure that's the right tier for what you need? — Ivan
